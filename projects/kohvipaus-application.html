Kohvipaus app

automation and programs 2025

I absolutely hate having to open up Google every day just to search for the electricity prices. I also cannot be bothered to read news from five different news portals every day (but I do like to get my information from several sources - to get a better overview of what is happening in the world, as well as see news from specific fields that interest me, like tech). That's why I made an application that can be accessed directly from the desktop with one click. It shows me all the info I need: the electricity prices for the next 24 hours and the top news from the sources of my own choice. Kohvipaus is available in two versions - night (black, purple tones) and latte (beige, brown).

Programs and methods used

  • Python
  • Tkinter (GUI)
  • Matplotlib (graphs)
  • Elering API (electricity prices)
  • RSS (news)
